Brand/Client

BAJAJ ELECTRICALS LIMITED

Objective

BAJAJ ELECTRICALS, a leading name in consumer products, Illumination and EPC, was looking at consolidating its position and engaging with large audiences in Mumbai.

Solution

Towards this, it was suggested that BAJAJ ELECTRICALS associates itself with the country’s largest cultural extragavanza, the KALA GHODA ARTS FESTIVAL. Multiple engagements were created for BAJAJ ELECTRICALS at the festival – MASTER CHEF COOK-OFFs, CHEF HUNTS, Workshops on Urban design and architecture that focussed on energy saving for commercial spaces etc.
Scintillating installations were created that best represented the brand and its USP.
Last, based on BAJAJ ELECTRICALS’ core philosophy of GET INSPIRED, an edit series titled as INSIPRED DAY was created, This series showcased the key highlights of each day of the KALA GHODA FESTIVAL for all nine days.

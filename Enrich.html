Brand/Client

Enrich Salon

Objective

Increasing Brand Salience of Enrich Salons in Mumbai, leading to increased consumer footfalls in the Salons

Solution

Mumbai is a key market for Enrich Salons. Therefore we set ourselves out to identify a marketing opportunity that would give the brand a chance to engage with lakhs of Mumbaikars. And what better than the Kala Ghoda Arts Festival! We proposed multiple activities for the brand at the popular festival with the objective to increasing brand affinity as well as promoting store visits. For eg, we set up a make-over booth on the main Kala Ghoda street offering complimentary make-overs to the festival visitors. Here, discount coupons were also distributed leading to increased store-footfalls at various locations of Enrich Salons. To enhance the Brand Salience, multiple celeb performers were given a touch-up before their performance by the Enrich Salon reps, and pictures posted of the celebs with a hamper from the brand. Additionally, all the Artists’ green-rooms were branded as Enrich Salon zones. All this led to great visibility and brand mileage for the Enrich Salons.

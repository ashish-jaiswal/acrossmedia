Our Services

Brand IPRs and Brand Properties

ACROSS MEDIA’s primary focus is creation of Brand IPRs that will grow in size and scale over time.

Brand Solutions

One of Marketers’ key roles is identifying and evaluating ideas that are suggested by their creative and media agencies. However, in-depth understanding of the Marketer’s business is lacking with these agencies. At ACROSS MEDIA SOLUTIONS, we deep-dive into client’s business, understanding it layer by layer, before suggesting marketing solutions to clients – solutions that are impactful, effective and optimal.

Influencer Marketing and Content Marketing

From organising a media drive for the auto press, to booking and managing a celebrity or an influencer-lead engagement program, ACROSS MEDIA SOLUTIONS has the connections and experience to connect you with PR and digital communities to get your brand message and product attributes out there.

Opportunity Scouting for Brands

At ACROSS MEDIA SOLUTIONS, we reach to clients with multiple association opportunities from different genres – from a Music event in Mumbai to a Food Fest in Delhi, from a fashion event in Bengaluru to an Art event in Kolkata – we track them all. This helps our clients to evaluate these multiple opportunities through a single-window called ACROSS MEDIA SOLUTIONS. Additionally, through our expertise and experience of dabbling with initiatives, we get this opportunities to clients at best prices and terms.
